WHAT IS "AARAMBH"?

"Aarambh" literally means "the Beginning", the start of your journey of civil services exam preparation. This is a highly curated course for the beginners and also for intermediate aspirants. This course aims to provide you a good orientation with a well defined guidance plan designed by best educators, mentors and successful candidates. Aarambh course covers the entire UPSC preparation strategy in a nutshell. It provides you a holistic approach for UPSC CSE at all levels, be it Prelims, Mains or Interview. It is aimed to ensure your success by helping you reach your desired position of a civil servant.

Why You Should Choose Aarambh ?

A lot of aspirants start their preparation with no orientation or primary knowledge about the exam. Many of them waste their precious time looking for guidance, sources and strategy. Aarambh course helps you save your time by providing you with everything that is necessary and required. Our educators and mentors are success driven individuals who are ready to guide you at every step in your journey of CSE. What to do ? What to study ? How to study ? From Where to study ? etc. are the questions which bothers every UPSC aspirant even in their dreams. Thus, we brought for you a holistically designed preparation course "Aarambh" which provides the right direction to your hard work and efforts. Aarambh covers prelims, mains, Interview preparation along with test series, personal guidance, mentorship and much more.

How Aarambh Course Helps You Succeed ?

Aarambh provides you the initial vision and correct orientation for UPSC Civil Service Examination. Aarambh tells you how to start your preparation in the right direction. Our educators are specialists in their fields and they designed one of the best strategy for each subject. Our Mentors, who are successful individuals will futher guide you at every stage covering all aspects of UPSC IAS examination. Join Aarambh course to kick-start your journey of comprehensive UPSC IAS exam preparation. Learn from best faculty and successful mentors. Aarambh keeps your preparation on track by taking regular tests and providing you constant feedbacks. You have to begin your journey in order to reach your destination. Join Aarambh !

When You Should Join Aarambh ?

"The Secret of getting ahead is getting started". Join today itself to start your preparation journey with us. There is nothing difficult in life provided your desire to succeed is strong enough. It's never too late to start. It is often said "Your attitude at the beginning determines your success and failure". So fasten your belts up and toil hardest to reach your desired destination. One year preparation with determined mindset and correct strategy with lot of hard work is all that is needed.
