1. Content Writing

Writing quality articles/analysis in a simple language.

If you think that you have the ability to make complicated things simple, you may create articles on current/conventional topics and send us.

If your article meets our quality guidelines, we will publish it.


Web Writing Guidelines for Content Writers:

1. Keep your audience in mind. The article should be original in nature (no plagiarism). You can do research and analysis on the topic (and give a proper reference), but not copy from anywhere.
2. Focus on a single topic and ensure that you cover all the aspects related to the same (eg: definition, history, different dimensions, positives, negatives, future, conclusion etc).
3. Prepare simple and easy to learn articles. The article should help an aspirant to learn that topic faster.
4. Use of bullet points, diagrams and mind maps are appreciated.
5. The total length of the article should be 1000-3000 words.
6. Send us images which can be used along with the content as separate attachments.
7. Take extra care to avoid spelling mistakes, grammar mistakes, punctuation mistakes, improper use of case etc. Use a grammar checker software and correct your mistakes before you send us.
8. Give proper reference in the end.
9. Send two write-ups in word format (.doc) along with a brief resume of yours clearly indicating your academic performance and experience with UPSC CSE, if any.
10. Send the new articles to support@opdemy.com with the subject line Article Submission: [Topic].

Things to note:

*Your articles would be published on our website and our monthly magazine which we would provide for free to everyone. This would reach lakhs of people and you have an opportunity to earn a name for yourself as a writer/content developer.
• The fewer corrections/editing needed in the articles, the faster the article will be published.
• There is no need of email follow-ups regarding the status of your articles. If your article is worth publishing in opdemy.com, we would do that. It’s almost impractical to reply to thousands of email we get.
• Articles from those who are already in service or those who have reached Civil Service Interview stage or written mains will be given higher weightage.
