Opdemy CSE Fellowship 2020

It is indeed great pleasure for Opdemy to announce its much awaited ‘Opdemy CSE Fellowship’ programme. This is going to be the flagship programme of Opdemy as an institution in the making. The programme is specially designed for the brightest aspirants appearing in UPSC CSE 2021.

Opdemy CSE Fellowship is a one-year highly selective personalized guidance programme for civil service aspirants. Opdemy fellows will be selected after a rigorous competitive process. Each fellow will receive free coaching, exclusive guidance, access to in-service officer mentors, free study materials and much more.

Vision and Mission

Opdemy CSE Fellowship Programme is curated to help the brightest young individuals aspiring to qualify UPSC civil services exam. Every year lakhs of aspirants appear for UPSC exam and hardly few make it to the final list. In a country like India, not everyone can afford premium coaching to prepare for such highly competitive exams. Also, it is hard for aspirants to manage the living expenses of metropolitan cities where some sort of guidance is available.

Thus, Opdemy has designed an online ‘CSE Fellowship’ programme where guidance will reach to an aspirant’s door. We aim to bridge the gap between education and affordability. Opdemy aspire to connect the humble aspirants with their dreams of becoming successful civil servants.

This fellowship is delivered with the help of successful candidates, experts, toppers, in-service and retired senior officers. We aim to build a more inclusive society by helping underprivileged aspirants to complete their dreams. We just wish to act as a small cog in the nation building process by creating the leaders of future.

Founder Speaks


Opdemy CSE fellowship programme is crafted to help the young brightest aspirants. It is to make them fully equipped in their preparation to face every single challenge thrown at them by UPSC. The Fellowship aims to accomplish an aspirant’s goal by providing him or her necessary sources and guidance for free of cost at their doorstep. This is one unique fellowship where serious aspirants will be given 24x7 access to personalized guidance from experts. It is a one year long mutual commitment between Opdemy and CSE Fellow to furnish positive results with hard-work, persistence and determination.

What fellowship provides you ?

Opdemy fellowship is a 24x7 commitment for 365 days or more, if required.

It is a one year long personalized coaching programme that will expose aspirants to a diverse set of strategies and critical thinking abilities.

The Fellowship covers your entire Pre + Mains + Interview needs.

Fellows and their preparation will be under the scanner 24x7 throughout the year.

Fellows will be rigorously supervised, guided and mentored by expert educators, academicians, and officers on daily basis.

High focus on improving writing skills for mains.

Fellows will receive free coaching, study materials, mock tests, guidance for filling DAF and all necessary grooming for the Interview.

Fellow’s preparation will be tracked by weekly and monthly tests.
