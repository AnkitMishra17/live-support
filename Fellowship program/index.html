Opdemy Fellowship Program

Ten 100% sponsored Fellowships.

Everything is covered.

These fellowships will cover the cost of all courses on Opdemy.com. They will also include cost of all the study materials, books, magazines etc. needed for the preparation of UPSC CSE

Exclusive Mentorship

Guidance by the Officers.

Winners of the fellowship would be guided exclusively by the team of Opdemy.com mentors and officers who have agreed to be part of this initiative.

Eligibility

These candidates would be preferred.

  • Candidates eligible to write UPSC CSE 2021.
  • Fresh graduates or in final year of college or going into final year.
  • English medium candidates.
  • Qualify our Fellowship test.

How to Apply

Just follow these steps

  • Register on www.opdemy.com
  • Send us your resume on support@opdemy.com.
  • Appear in the test.
  • Wait for declaration of results.
